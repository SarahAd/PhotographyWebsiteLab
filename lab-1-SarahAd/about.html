Ever since I was a little girl, I was facinated with art. It was always my favorite class at school, as I loved to see and create beauty. Now, as a photographer, I get to live this passion everyday! I love to find and photograph beautiful places both in the natural and architectural worlds, and capture photos of people and thier joy in candid moments. I always love to meet someone new, and help them find a place that they find beautiful where I can capture photographs of them that they will love. I hope you will reach out to me so we can begin!
